Privacy Policy - Happy Block

Last updated: 2026-01-15

Thank you for playing Happy Block (the “App”), developed by jiguanghuyu. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect and handle information when you use our App, including in-app purchases.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require users to register or provide personal information such as name, email, or phone number.

However, the App may collect information automatically through third-party services, including:

  • Device information (device model, OS version, language)
  • Advertising identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID)
  • App usage data (session duration, gameplay progress)
  • Crash logs and performance diagnostics

Additionally, if you make in-app purchases via Google Play Billing, Google may collect:

  • Payment transaction details (amount, currency, item purchased)
  • Google account information necessary to process the purchase

Note: We do not store or process your payment details. All payment information is handled securely by Google.

2. How We Use Information

Collected information is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Displaying advertisements (Unity Ads / IAA)
  • Measuring ad performance
  • Analyzing gameplay behavior
  • Improving game stability and user experience
  • Debugging and crash analysis
  • Processing in-app purchases through Google Play Billing

We do not sell or share personal data for our own purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Unity Ads / IAA – for in-app advertising
  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics – for analytics and crash reporting
  • Google Play Billing – for processing in-app purchases

These services may collect data according to their own privacy policies. We encourage users to review their policies.

4. Children’s Privacy

Our App is not specifically directed at children under 13 years of age.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will remove it promptly.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information processed by the App. However, no electronic storage or transmission method is completely secure. Payment information is securely handled by Google and not stored by us.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page and take effect immediately.
